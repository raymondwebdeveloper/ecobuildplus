Why Chicago Trusts Our Basement Remodeling

A basement remodel shouldn’t feel like an afterthought; it should be the absolute highlight of your home. While other contractors see a dark storage space, we see your future luxury home theater, custom wet bar, or private guest suite.

EcoBuild Plus doesn’t just “finish” basements; we engineer them. We take the complex challenges of lower-level construction (moisture, lighting, and airflow) and turn them into our strengths. We deliver a space so refined, you’ll forget you’re underground.

Here is how we build the basement of your dreams:

  • •

    Visionary Space Planning: We don’t just put up walls; we curate flow. We design open-concept layouts that seamlessly integrate with the rest of your home, turning wasted square footage into high-value real estate.

  • •

    Fortified Engineering: A dream basement must be a dry basement. We utilize advanced moisture mitigation and superior insulation systems to guarantee your investment is protected against dampness, mold, and cold forever.

  • •

    Atmosphere & Light Mastery: We eradicate the “dungeon” feel entirely. Through strategic egress window installation and layered LED lighting plans, we create a bright, airy environment that rivals your main floor’s comfort.

  • •

    Limitless Customization: If you can dream it, we can build it. From soundproof home recording studios to spa-grade bathrooms and gourmet kitchenettes, we handle the complex mechanicals others shy away from.

  • •

    Precision Project Management: Basement renovations require strict code compliance. We manage every permit and inspection with military precision, ensuring your electrical, plumbing, and HVAC systems are flawless and safe.

  • •

    The “Forever” Standard: We use materials built to last. We don’t cut corners on the things you can’t see, ensuring your finished basement remains pristine and functional for generations.

Don’t trust your home’s foundation to amateurs. Many contractors can hang drywall, but few can execute a luxury basement renovation that adds true, lasting value to your home. We set the standard for quality, and we refuse to compromise.
