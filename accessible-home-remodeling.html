Barrier-Free Entryways & Hallway Mobility

Seamless movement is essential for a safe home. EcoBuild Plus eliminates structural bottlenecks, like steep steps and narrow halls, to create effortless flow from the curb to every room. We connect your home with accessible entry solutions designed for safety and ease:

  • •

    Zero-Step Access: We install custom wheelchair ramps and gently sloped walkways to ensure a safe, barrier-free entrance for everyone.

  • •

    Accessible Doorways: We widen frames to 36+ inches for wheelchair clearance and install lever-style handles to replace hard-to-turn knobs, aiding those with arthritis or grip limitations.

  • •

    Smart & Safe Navigation: Enhance visibility and ease with motion-activated lighting, automatic door openers, and continuous non-slip flooring to minimize tripping hazards.

Connect every room with confidence. Our modifications ensure you can move through your home freely, without obstacles or boundaries.

Frequently asked questions

How Do You Make a Safe House Still Look Stylish and Not Like a Hospital?

You make a safe house look stylish and not like a hospital by using Universal Design principles that focus on luxury over medical necessity. We build luxury curbless entry showers and adaptive kitchens that look straight out of design magazines to create a beautiful environment.

Can You Integrate Safety Features Into a High-End Design So They Are Barely Noticeable?

We integrate safety features into a high-end design so they are barely noticeable by blending invisible accessibility with wide open flows. We use hidden structural reinforcements to support your needs without looking institutional so your home remains a stylish sanctuary.

How Do Lights That Turn on by Themselves Help Keep My Family Safe at Night?

Lights that turn on by themselves help keep your family safe at night by providing motion-activated visibility in bathrooms and hallways. These smart navigation tools reduce tripping hazards and help everyone move through the home without searching for switches.

How Do You Fix a Kitchen So Someone in a Wheelchair Can Still Cook Dinner?

We fix a kitchen so someone in a wheelchair can still cook dinner by installing lowered countertops and roll-under sinks. We redesign traditional layouts into accessible workspaces that include smart storage like pull-out shelves to maximize reach and independence.

Is There a Way to Make a Shower Easy to Get Into Without a Big Step?

There is a way to make a shower easy to get into without a big step by building a zero-threshold curbless entry. This design replaces dangerous tub edges with a barrier-free entrance that allows for seated bathing and wheelchair access.

How Do You Make the Bathroom Floor Safe So Nobody Slips When It’s Wet?

You make the bathroom floor safe so nobody slips when it is wet by using slip-resistant materials and non-slip flooring. These surfaces provide stable movement in wet areas to mitigate fall risks for everyone in your home.

What Are “Grab Bars,” and Can They Actually Look Like Part of the Design?

Grab bars are supportive safety features that can look like part of the design when we use ADA compliant luxury models. We blend these bars with high-end fixtures to support mobility without creating a clinical or hospital look.

Is It Really Cheaper to Fix My Home Than to Move Into a Care Home?

It is cheaper to fix your home than to move into a care home because accessible remodeling is a one-time investment in your freedom. Remodeling costs a fraction of recurring care facility fees and helps you stay in the home you love.

How Long Does It Take to Add a Ramp or Fix the Entrance for a Wheelchair?

To add a ramp or fix the entrance for a wheelchair, our team manages the project through a four-step path to finish the work quickly. We install custom wheelchair ramps and gently sloped walkways to provide zero-step access to your home.

Can You Fix the House Now for Things We Might Need 10 Years Later?

We fix the house now for things you might need 10 years later by future-proofing your home with proactive adaptability. We modify your space to suit evolving mobility needs so you never face structural barriers in your own sanctuary.
