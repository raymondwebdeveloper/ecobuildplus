The 4-Step Process

Discovery & Planning

Before a shovel hits the ground, we define success. We collaborate with you and your architect to review plans, identify engineering challenges, and develop a detailed scope and budget. This crucial phase sets the foundation for a smooth build.

Selections & Materials

We guide you through the selection of finishes, fixtures, and materials, ensuring every detail aligns with your aesthetic vision and our high-performance standards. Long-lead items are ordered early to prevent delays.

Build & Project Management

Construction begins under the watchful eye of a dedicated Project Manager. You receive regular, transparent communication and photo updates via our cloud-based portal, keeping you informed without the stress of daily site visits.

Delivery & Sustainment

We don’t just hand over the keys; we ensure you understand your new home. We conduct a thorough walkthrough, provide comprehensive manuals for all systems, and remain your partner for future maintenance and care.

Frequently Asked Questions

How Is a Home Kept Clean and Livable During a Major Remodel?

During a major remodel, your home is kept clean and livable by utilizing a “Multi Barrier” strategy to isolate construction zones. This involves using ZipWall sealing systems and HEPA filtered air scrubbers to capture airborne particles, while work areas are physically sealed off from the “white zone” of your living space to protect indoor air quality.

Who Will Be My Main Person to Talk to During Construction?

The main person you will talk to during construction is your Project Manager who serves as your single point of contact. You receive regular progress updates and schedule oversight through our cloud based portal, which will ensure transparent communication without the need for constant site visits.

How Do You Protect My Existing Flooring and Furniture From Construction Dust?

Existing flooring and furniture are protected from construction dust by using non damaging ZipWall poles with non slip foot grips and padded top plates to secure protective sheeting without marking floors or ceilings. High traffic walkways are covered, and all work areas are cleared of debris daily to maintain a safe, walkable environment.

What Is the Process for Managing Noise and Neighbor Disruptions?

The process for managing noise and neighbor disruptions involves strictly adhering to strict Chicago working hours (typically 8 AM to 6 PM) and providing neighboring homes with formal letters detailing our timeline and contact information. Noisy activities like demolition are scheduled mid day to reduce the impact on those working from home.

Do I Need to Be Home Every Day to Let the Construction Crews In?

You do not need to be home every day to let construction crews in because site access is managed rigorously through our secure project management system. You can track daily site activity and labor updates remotely via our cloud based portal, which provides full visibility of the project without requiring your presence on site.

How Soon Can We Start Once the Architectural Plans Are Finalized?

Once architectural plans are finalized, we can start the Selections & Materials phase immediately, ordering long lead items early to prevent scheduling delays. We also navigate the Chicago permitting process during this time so construction can start the moment approvals are secured.

What Happens if We Find Unexpected Structural Issues After Demolition Starts?

If unexpected structural issues are found after demolition starts, our team stops work to review the findings with you and your architect. We provide a revised scope and transparent cost adjustment before proceeding to make sure your home remains safe and the budget is protected.

Will I Receive Regular Photo Updates of the Progress if I Am Out of Town?

Regular photo updates of the progress will be provided if you are out of town through our integrated project management portal. These real time snapshots allow you to monitor construction milestones and maintain total visibility of the project from any location.

How Are Construction Schedules Managed During Extreme Chicago Winter Weather?

Construction schedules are managed during extreme Chicago winter weather by prioritizing interior projects like kitchen or bathroom remodeling during the coldest months to keep your project moving regardless of outdoor conditions. For structural additions, we use heated tenting and frost prevention to protect materials like concrete and masonry from freezing.

What Kind of Support or Warranty Is Included After a Remodel Is Finished?

The kind of support or warranty included after a remodel is finished consists of a 3 year limited labor warranty for conventional construction and an extended 5 year warranty for Passive House projects. Additionally, we conduct a final walkthrough to hand over comprehensive system manuals and remain your long term partner for future home maintenance.
