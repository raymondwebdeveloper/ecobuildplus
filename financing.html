Frequently asked questions

What are the best financing options for fast home upgrades or small remodels?

The best financing options for fast home upgrades or small remodels include our No-Interest & Short-Term Relief Plans, which offer interest-free periods of 6 or 12 months. These plans are specifically designed for quick updates, allowing you to pay zero interest if the balance is cleared within the promotional window.

Can I get interest-free financing for a larger, long-term renovation?

You can get interest-free financing for a larger, long-term renovation by utilizing our 18-month no-interest plan. This option provides extra time for more extensive projects, allowing you to keep your initial financing cost at zero while you complete high-impact structural updates.

What is the most flexible payment plan for large-scale home improvements?

The most flexible payment plan for large-scale home improvements is our 15-year fixed-rate investment plan, which offers an APR of 12.99%. This long-term solution keeps monthly payments as low as possible, making it a sustainable choice for major builds or total home transformations.

What are the current interest rates for mid-size or major renovation projects?

The current interest rates for mid-size or major renovation projects typically range from 6.99% to 9.99% APR depending on the selected term length. For example, 5-year plans offer our most competitive fixed rate of 6.99% for balanced investments, while 10-year plans sit at 9.99% to provide predictable monthly payments for decade-long security.

How does “Fast Track Approval” help my construction project start sooner?

“Fast Track Approval” helps your construction project start sooner by providing streamlined financial approvals that allow our team to break ground without waiting on traditional bank delays. By aligning your budget with a pre-approved plan during our master blueprint phase, we can order long-lead materials immediately to keep your timeline on track.
